[1]Andũ mwĩmenyeriei gwĩtigĩraNgai mwene hinya wothe.Inyuĩ mwĩmenyeriei gwĩtigĩraũrĩa wĩna muoyo na ũhoti.[2]Wendo wake nĩ mũnene,Tha ciake nĩ cĩyũrĩte biũOna marakara no tagũo.Wakorwo ndũkũigua watho wa Roho.[3]Watho wake nĩ mũtheru,Na we wĩtherie ũhane take,Warega kũmenya ndeto ciake,Nĩ kurĩ na hinya atwarane nawe.[4]Ndeto ciake coimire igũrũ,Nacio ciaku no cia gukũ thĩHatarĩ na ũhoti wa MwathaniŨndũ ũcio wĩ hinya ndungĩhoteka.[5]Maitho make nĩmaroraga,Ciana ciake cia gĩkeno,Ona matũ make nĩmekũigua.Mahoya ma ciana ciake cia gĩkeno.[6]Nake nĩamahete hinya,Wa kũhota mbara ya thĩ-ĩno.Na yathira mambate igũrũ,Makahurũke thĩna ũyũ wa thĩ-ĩno.
