[1]Hitho ciothe na ũthamaki,Na ũtonga wothe wa thĩ ĩnoMuthenya ũmwe nĩ ĩgathira.[2]Mũthenya ũcio Mũraika wa iriganĩria,Nĩakeherio ngoro cĩa andũ iririkane,Uhoro wama ũrĩa cieragwo.[3]Arĩa a gũkũ thĩ makonaJesũ agiũka na ũthamaki.Hĩndĩ ĩyo mĩago na ĩkenoCiothe cia thĩ nĩ ĩgathira.[4]Ndũriri cĩothe cĩa thĩ-ĩno.Nĩikagirĩka na kĩgiIthenya rĩa mĩago na ikeno, Ciothe iria merĩragĩria.[5]Mũgwĩtwo inyuothe na ihenya,Rĩu ihinda no ta rĩthiru,Mũnyaka nĩ ũrĩa ũgwĩtwoNake oke kwĩ Jesũ erire.[6]Mũgwĩtwo ĩnyuothe na ĩhenya,Rĩu ihĩnda no ta rĩthiru.Mũnyaka nĩ ũrĩa ũgwitikiaMũthenya ũcĩo ũtanakinya.[7]Mũkwĩruo wĩthagathage,Mũthenya ũkĩrie gũkinya.Aingĩ anyu makorererwoNa mĩhango ya gũkũ thĩ.[8]Mũthenya ũrĩa ciira ũkaguũrioMothiũ-inĩ ma ndũrĩrĩ.Mĩhang’o na mĩago na ikeno.Cĩa thĩ ĩno nĩ igathira.[9]Arĩa atherũ mũthenya ũcio,Mũrango nĩ ũkahingũrũo.Nĩgetha maheo ikeno,Na tanji meriragĩria.
