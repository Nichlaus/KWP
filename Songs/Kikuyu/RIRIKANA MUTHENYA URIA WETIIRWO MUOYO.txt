[1]Ririkana mũthenya ũria wetĩirwo muoyo nĩ Roho,Waiguire wendo ngoro tigaga kũriganĩrwo.Ririkana muoyo ũtige thieya na mawĩra wanarutaNa ng’aragu wĩhotorete njohero tigaga kuriganĩrwo.[2]Wakirĩrĩrie kũmenwo na ũgĩkirĩrĩria mbaara,Na gĩcambanio kĩnene tigaga kũriganĩrwo.[3]Nĩ mehia kuriganĩrwo ũrĩa waiguiri o mbereJesu nĩ muoyo agwĩtĩire tigaga kũriganĩrwo.[4]Rũgendo-inĩ rwaku ruothe, gũkirĩrĩria ndũgate,Na ndũgate ũumĩrĩru tigaga kũriganĩrwo.[5]Ng’endo cia wĩra wa Ngai cia mĩnoga na thithinoTi cia tũhũ wahotana tigaga kũriganĩrwo.[6]Utũkũ kwarahũkaga, bbara ya toro ũgatoria,Roho waku ũthaithe Ngai, tigaga kũriganĩrwo.
