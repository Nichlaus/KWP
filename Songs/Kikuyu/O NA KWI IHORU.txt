[1]O na kwĩ ihoru no ndagĩũka,Ona njiyũrĩtwo nĩ kĩeha,Nĩ njũũĩ harĩa ũnjigĩteNdĩmũgũthaithi ũronjigua[2]Kĩeha na ihoru cianjiyura,Ndaririkana wĩra waku,Ũrĩa ũtegeirwo nĩ mwĩkĩ naĩ,Akĩenda kũũthahia ũthukie.[3]Wambe ũririkane mũrũguo,Ũgĩcokerera mũndũ thĩ,Nĩ getha ũrĩngwo nĩ tha Mũrungu,Ndũkandekererĩe mwak-inĩ.[4]Wĩ mũrĩithi wa rũru rwaku,Wĩ mwaki mwĩgunyi wa ciaku,Nĩ gũkorwo ikĩonja nĩũmenyaga,Ona igĩtũrũha no ũciine.[5]Mũndũ wa thĩ nĩwamethahia,Egũmagĩra na mwĩĩro,Ndũgatindĩrĩre matarania,Na ndũkagere manjĩtĩka.[6]Kĩone nĩo aya mahoreire,Ometereire kiugo gĩaku,Magĩtegeire matũ makĩigue,Wendo waku ũrĩa ũtariĩ.[7]Kĩigue ũrĩa matumũka moiga,Mambararĩtie moko mao,Makwarĩire hĩ ciao ũkĩmahe,Kĩrĩa ũkuona kimagĩrĩire.[8]Nĩ macoka moina ndu ciao,Rĩu no gũkũgathĩrĩria,Nĩũndũ wametĩka nawamomĩrĩria makĩria.[9]Gĩtũũre Baba ũkumuthaga,Kĩama gĩkĩ weta gĩa thayũ,Nĩ getha mothiũ monana,Ũndũ wa metha ũkaga.
