[1]Mwathani ĩgũa tha ndikahotwo.Nĩ ngoma rũgendo-inĩ.Nĩ rwa ngaragu na nyota,Mwathani ndũthaitire.[2]Ngĩona thĩna na magerio,Mwathani wakwa e mbere.Ndigacoka gwa Shaitani, Njĩrĩgĩrĩire muoyo.[3]Ingĩmenwo nĩ arata otheO hamwe na aciari akwa.Ndĩnengerete Mũkũri.Anjĩke ũrĩa e kwenda.[4]Roho wakwa mwarĩrĩria.Ndũkandige ndĩ thĩ ĩno.Nĩgũkorwo ndĩ o mũthĩni,Ongoro yakwa thĩ-inĩ.[5]Nĩngenaga mũno mũnoNĩkũnjĩurĩa Roho.Ũrogocwo ũrokumioNĩ irĩndĩ iria ikwendete.[6]Mũhunokia Jesu witũ.Nĩagoka na mũmenyeTondũ tĩ mũrũ wa Adamu.Ahingagia ciĩranĩro[7]Nguuga shaitani arohotwo.Mũcukani ũrĩ hitho.Nĩgetha tũthiĩ rũgendoTũgikĩnye Zayuni.[8]Gĩkeno tũkona igũrũ.Twarĩkia kũhotana.Nake shaitani na andũ ake.Maikio mwaki-inĩ mũũru.[9]Mwathani igua tha cia andũMakũmenye ũrĩa ũtariĩ.Na merire mehia narua,Utanacoka rĩngĩ.[10]Iyũrĩa arĩa makwendete,Roho waku Mũtheru.Nĩguo mahoote Shaitani,Mwene ndũma na hitho.
