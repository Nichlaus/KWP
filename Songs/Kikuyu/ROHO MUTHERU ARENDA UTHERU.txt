[1]Roho mũtheru arenda ũtheru wĩtherie.Waga gwĩtheria nĩagũgũtiganĩria.[2]Menyerera Roho Mutheru waku.Ikĩro-inĩ ciothe wanamũhũrithia.Mũgũtongoria ndakanagũcirithie.[3]Wirutanĩrie mũno ũtonye igũrũ,Mwĩri ũrehĩta ũkoiga nama ndũngĩthiĩ.Naguo muoyo ũkoiga ngatũũra igũrũ,Muoyo ũgakena mũno wahota mwĩrĩ.[4]Wĩmenyerereĩ matukũ maya,Nĩmo marathirwo nĩ Anabii a tene.Nĩ matukũ moru mena mĩhang’o,Mũthaithe Roho mũno amũhungurie.[5]Gĩthethũko kĩrĩ na Atũmwo igũrũ,Hamwe na Anabii ningĩ na Araika.Makĩũrania Roho kaĩ ũkambata rĩ?Waganu ũrĩa ũrĩa thĩ ũitĩrĩrio mwaki?[6]Nake Roho akoiga ndigaĩrie hanini,Kũrĩ ngoro itigairie kũrutwo nduma-inĩ.Naniĩ gĩthethũko nĩkĩnginyĩrĩire,Hinge wĩra wakwa thiĩ ngahurũke.[7]Roho ekuga matukũ nĩ mathiru,Makũmũthaitha mũikare ũtheru-inĩNĩekuhinga wĩra ainũke akahuruke.
