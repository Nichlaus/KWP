[1]Thikĩrĩriai mũigue mũmenye,Gũkũ thĩ kwĩna ndũma nene,Tondũ shaitani nĩ ehĩtĩte,Nĩekũgarũra ngoro hũthũ.Umĩrĩrĩai mũnyite watho,Ciana cianyu ionye muoyo-inĩ,Tondũ kwĩna thĩna mũnene,O tomĩrĩrio nĩ ngoro hũthũ.[2]Ngumũthaĩtha mũhoe nama, Mũhumbwo ũtheru na wendanĩ,Nĩ getha mbaara yokira,Mũhotanire cĩana cĩanyu.[3]Mũiyũrwo nĩtha na maithori,Tondũ wa mĩĩro ĩiyũire ngoro,Ya mawega ona mĩhango,Ya kũgarũra ngoro hũthũ.[4]Maũndũ marĩa nyonaga thĩ,Nĩmakũgocithia ithe witũ,Ndarĩkia wĩra wa ithe witũ,Ngainũkia irĩithia kũu igũrũ.[5]Ngainũkia irĩithia kũu ĩgũrũ,Nĩngagathĩrĩrio matu-inĩ,Mbere ya gĩtĩ gĩa ithe witũ.
