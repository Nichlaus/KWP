[1]Arĩa megũthambia nguo,Na thakame ya Mwathani;Megũtongorio nĩ Roho.Arĩa othe athinguNĩo magathera,Na ũtherĩ ũhana wa riũa,Rĩrĩa magakinya igũrũKũrĩa ĩthe witu athanaga.[2]Mwathani nĩakahingũra,Nyũmba ĩrĩa ĩtagwo therũ,Mũromo-inĩ nĩ ũhonokio,Na itĩ ciayo nĩ ũthamaki.[3]Arĩa matũraga igũrũ,Mekũria aya moĩmite kũũ,Aya me na nguo njerũMarigĩcĩirie Mwathani?[4]Moimĩte minyamaro-inĩ,Magethura mĩoyo yao,O na matũirwo kũragwoMatiehereire Mwathani.[5]Nĩcio ndũngata cia RohoCia kuga thĩ kwĩ mũtino;Mekũgayana ũthamakiHamwe na Mwathani wao.
