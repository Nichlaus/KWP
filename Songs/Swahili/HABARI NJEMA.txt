[1]Habari njema twaisikia,Kila mahali duniani, “duniani”Nawe mwenzangu waisikia,Ama u bado sikiliza.Wanahubiri watu wa MunguNa wewe mwenzangu wajivuna! “wajivuna”Wajidanganya na ulimwengu,Ambao kesho watoweka.[2]Habari njema yasema kwamba,Huko mbinguni kwake Mungu “kwake Mungu”Hakuna taabu uzima tele,Wala sumbuko hutapata.[3]Yesu asema asiyesikia,Hata na mimi simjui! “sijui”Nikiwadia nitamwambiaOndoka kwangu sikujui.
